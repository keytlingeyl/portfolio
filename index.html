About Me

Hello! I'm Caitlin Gail D. Abizar, a 2nd-year Computer Science student with a passion for where technology meets creativity. As a curious and innovative individual, I'm constantly exploring the intersection of code and design, where functionality meets aesthetics.

With a strong foundation in computer science, I'm well-versed in programming languages such as []. However, my interests extend beyond the realm of code. I'm also an art enthusiast with a flair for design, and I believe that a beautiful user experience is just as important as a well-written algorithm.

In my free time, you can find me experimenting with graphic design, photography, or digital art. I'm fascinated by the way design can influence human behavior and perception, and I'm always looking for ways to incorporate my creative skills into my technical projects.

Through this e-portfolio, I aim to showcase my technical skills, design sensibilities, and creative projects that bring together the best of both worlds. I'm excited to share my journey with you and explore the possibilities of where technology and art collide.
